Use YoGoose in Your URL Bar

One-time setup to replace Google in your address bar:

  • Open Chrome Settings
  • Go to Search engine
  • Click Manage search engines
  • Under "Site search," click Add
  • Name: Yogoose
  • Shortcut: yogoose.com
  • URL: https://yogoose.com/results.html?q=%s
  • Click Add, then click the 3 dots next to it and select Make default

Bonus: New tabs already open Yogoose. You can also type yg in the address bar as a shortcut.
