YoGoose

Use YoGoose in Your URL Bar

Takes 30 seconds. All steps stay visible so you don't lose them.

1

Open Chrome's search engine settings

Copy this link, paste it in your address bar, and hit Enter:

chrome://settings/searchEngines
2

Scroll down to "Site search" and click Add

NOT the "Search engines" section at the top — scroll further down to "Site search" and click Add.

Fill in these three fields (copy each one):

Name Yogoose
Shortcut yogoose.com
URL https://yogoose.com/results.html?q=%s

Then click Add to save it.

3

Make Yogoose your default

Find Yogoose in the Site search list. Click the three dots (…) next to it and select "Make default".

✓

That's it!

Now every search in your address bar goes through Yogoose. Type "weather" or "youtube" to try it out.
