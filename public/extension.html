Use YoGoose in Your URL Bar

Replace Google in your address bar. Every search — whether you want a website or an AI answer — goes through Yogoose.

1

Open your search engine settings

Click the button below — it'll take you right there.

Open Search Settings

If the button doesn't work, copy this into your address bar: chrome://settings/searchEngines

2

Click "Add" under Site Search

Scroll down to "Site search" and click the Add button. Then fill in:

Name: Yogoose
Shortcut: yogoose.com
URL: https://yogoose.com/results.html?q=%s
3

Make it your default

Find Yogoose in the list, click the three dots (…) next to it, and select "Make default".

✓

You're all set!

Now just type anything in your address bar — Yogoose will take it from here.

Try typing "weather" or "youtube" to see the magic.
