Use YoGoose in Your URL Bar

Replace Google in your address bar. Every search — whether you want a website or an AI answer — goes through Yogoose.

1

Open Chrome search settings

Go to Settings → Search engine → Manage search engines and site search

2

Add Yogoose as a search engine

Click Add under "Site search" and enter:

Name: Yogoose
Shortcut: yogoose.com
URL:

https://yogoose.com/results.html?q=%s
3

Set as default

Click the three dots next to Yogoose and select Make default. Done.

That's it. Every time you type in your address bar, Yogoose will instantly know whether to take you to a website or give you an AI answer. No more switching between Google and ChatGPT.
